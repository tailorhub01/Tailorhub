Privacy Policy

At Tailor Hub, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Delivery addresses
  • Order history and preferences
  • Payment information (securely processed)

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns
  • Device information
  • Location data (with permission)
  • Service preferences

Camera Permission

Camera Access

Our app requests camera permission (android.permission.CAMERA) to provide the following features:

  • Capture images for measurements and sizing
  • Take profile pictures
  • Upload product images

Important: Camera Access Controls

  • Camera access is ONLY used when you explicitly open the camera feature within the app
  • We do NOT capture photos or videos in the background
  • We do NOT record silently or without your knowledge
  • Camera permission is requested only when you choose to use camera-related features
  • You can revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings

Image Handling

  • Images you capture are stored locally on your device or uploaded only with your explicit action
  • We do NOT share your images with third parties
  • Images are used solely for the purpose you intended (measurements, profile, products)
  • You have full control over which images are uploaded and stored

How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order updates and notifications
  • Improve our services
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal requirements

Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With service providers (delivery partners, payment processors)
  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With your explicit consent

Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure payment processing through trusted providers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability
  • File a complaint with authorities
